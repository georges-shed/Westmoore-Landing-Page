Limited Liability Partnership

£69.99+VAT

Buy Now

Our LLP formation package is designed to take care of all the key steps required to correctly register a limited liability partnership in the UK.

An LLP is most suitable for any type of business that would work as a partnership, but an LLP is registered when the partners want to have the protection of limited liability.

LLP’s give partners flexibility in how the LLP should be managed and create a separate legal entity enabling perpetual life of the LLP , certain taxation benefits and of course creating a credible professional image.

Typically, we will have your LLP registered the same day (we are dependent on Companies House workload at the time of application!).

Our package includes the LLP Certificate, digital copies of all LLP documents and a free draft LLP agreement template for your use. You also get access to our cloud based Company Manager portal to maintain your LLP’s details.
